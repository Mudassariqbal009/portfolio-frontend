Last updated: January 1, 2026 · Effective: January 1, 2026

CloudOps Agency ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect through our website and contact form, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

We do not collect any data automatically (no analytics cookies, no tracking pixels) unless you explicitly consent.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To respond to your enquiry

3. Third-Party Processors

Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io). By submitting the form, your data is transmitted to Formspree's servers and subject to their Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We retain contact enquiries for up to 24 months. You may request deletion at any time by emailing hello@cloudops.agency.

5. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to: access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@cloudops.agency.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email hello@cloudops.agency.
